Welcome to KiNECT

Our Mission:

Our group has noticed within society opportunities to give back to our various communities can be difficult to come by. Nonetheless, educational, corporate, and government institutions, often require students to fulfill hours of community service. Individuals often find it hard to interact with their communities because their interest is not reflected in their options and there is no centralized way to find opportunities. Our project is to streamline the way that all people find volunteer opportunities by compiling an organizations into one place using tech.
