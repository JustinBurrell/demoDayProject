Welcome to KiNECT

Our Mission

Opportunities to give back to our various communities can be difficult to come by. Furthermore, individuals who wish to give back often find it hard to seek opportunities due to the lack of a centralized platform. Our project is designed to streamline the search for volunteer opportunities by compiling organizations in one place via tech.
